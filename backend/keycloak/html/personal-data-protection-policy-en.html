Personal Data Protection Policy

Sample Holdings (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") understands the importance of protecting personal information and recognizes that it is the Company's important responsibility to handle personal information properly.

  1. The Company will comply with laws, regulations, and other norms regarding the protection of personal information, and will establish and implement rules and specific regulations regarding the use and provision of personal information.
  2. When the Company obtains personal information, it will clarify the purpose of use, limit the scope of use of the collected personal information, and handle it appropriately.
  3. The Company will take appropriate measures to prevent the loss, alteration, or leakage of personal information.
  4. If the Company receives a request for disclosure, correction, or deletion of the information of an individual, it will promptly respond to the request in accordance with laws and regulations.
  5. The Company will establish, maintain, and continuously improve a compliance program to protect personal information.
